Welcome to Samadhan

Authentic Konkani Hospitality at the Edge of the Ocean

Nestled in the heart of Malvan, Samadhan Resort is a family-friendly oasis. We pride ourselves on offering a unique "Sea Touch" experience. Located just 2.5 km from the city center, you can step out of your room directly onto the soft sands of Wairy Beach.

Whether you are here for the thrilling water sports, the peaceful sunsets, or our mouth-watering homemade Malvani cuisine, we ensure your stay is memorable and comfortable.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the nearest beach?

We are located exactly at Wairy/Tarkarli beach. Distance is Zero (Sea touch). Devbag is 3km away.

How to reach from Mumbai?

You can arrive by train to Kudal Stn (30km), Sindhudurg Stn, or Kankavli. Alternatively, use Chipi Airport or Bus/Travels. We provide pickup services via rental cars.

Is food provided?

Yes, we serve authentic homemade Malvani Veg and Non-veg food. Jain options are available on request. Outside food is not allowed.

What is the booking & cancellation policy?

A 50% advance payment is required for booking. Cancellation must be made at least 7 days before your booking date.
