Founded by Mr. Ravindra Patkar

A Legacy of Warmth & Hospitality

Under the visionary leadership of our owner, Mr. Ravindra Patkar, Samadhan Beach Resort has grown into one of Malvan's most beloved sea-touch destinations. What started as a dream to share the untouched beauty of Wairy Beach has blossomed into a resort famous across the region for its unparalleled service.

The entire Patkar family is deeply rooted in the daily operations of the resort. When you stay with us, you are experiencing authentic Konkani culture. From ensuring your room is immaculate to guiding you toward the best local adventures, our family is dedicated to making your stay perfect.

A Taste of Malvan

Famous For Our Food

The Patkar family is renowned for preparing delicious, mouth-watering meals that capture the true essence of coastal India.

Authentic Seafood

Malvan is synonymous with spectacular seafood, and our kitchen is at the heart of it. We source the freshest daily catch directly from local fishermen. Prepared using our secret family recipes and traditional Malvani spices, our Fish Thali, Prawns Fry, and Crab Masala are legendary among our guests.

  • ✓ Fresh daily ocean catch
  • ✓ Traditional Patkar family recipes
  • ✓ Authentic Konkani spices
🥗

Pure Vegetarian Options

We understand and respect the diverse dietary needs of all our guests. Samadhan Resort offers a dedicated, strictly Pure Vegetarian menu. From delicious Veg Thalis and Modak to custom Jain food preparations, we ensure the highest standards of purity and taste without compromise.

  • ✓ Separate Pure Veg preparations
  • ✓ Jain food available on request
  • ✓ Healthy, homely, and delicious
